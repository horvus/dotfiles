Summary

X Video Explorer, maker of modelines.

Automates calculations for generating X video modelines. This program is meant for the rare cases where autoconfiguartion via EDID fails and you don't already have a functioning modeline that can be tweaked with xvidtune(1) or kvideogen(1) or the like; it should get you to where you can use those tools.
