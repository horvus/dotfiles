StreamingConferences: Introduction

Introduction

Streaming events with Free Software requires an investment of time, patience and energy, in the form of testing. However the decision to use Free Software doesn’t mean to limit your abilities in streaming, it just requires a commitment to:

  1. time

  2. patience

  3. testing

Sending high quality clean streams using Free Software can only be achieved with the individual investment listed above. This will likely be a test in your patience, (even if you are a free software expert).
